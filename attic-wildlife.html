Useful request details

Information that can help providers understand the concern.

Sounds

When activity is heard

Mention whether sounds happen during the day, evening, overnight, or early morning.

Location

Where signs appear

Describe whether the concern seems near the attic, roofline, vents, soffits, chimney, or exterior wall.

Visual signs

What is visible

Droppings, odor, insulation movement, stains, nesting material, or exterior gaps can be useful to mention.

Photos

Safe image context

If safe, exterior photos of possible entry areas may help providers understand the request before follow-up.
