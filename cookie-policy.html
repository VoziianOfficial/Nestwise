Platform notice

Nestwise is a matching platform. It does not perform wildlife removal services directly.

1. Overview

Nestwise may use cookies and similar technologies to operate this website, remember user choices, support request forms, understand website performance, improve usability, and help process matching-related interactions.

Cookies are small files or pieces of data stored on a browser or device. Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, tracking pixels, analytics tags, and browser-based identifiers.

2. Types of cookies we may use

Nestwise may use different categories of cookies depending on website functionality, compliance needs, and platform operations.

  • Essential cookies: These support basic website functionality, page navigation, security, form behavior, and policy consent choices.
  • Preference cookies: These may remember settings or choices, such as whether a privacy notice was accepted or declined.
  • Analytics cookies: These may help understand how visitors use the website, which pages are visited, and how the browsing experience can be improved.
  • Marketing or lead-processing technologies: These may help understand referral sources, measure campaign performance, or support matching request routing.

3. Local storage and policy consent

This website may use browser local storage to remember whether a visitor accepted or declined the policy notice. This helps prevent the notice from appearing repeatedly after a choice has been made.

Clearing browser data, using private browsing, or switching devices may reset this preference.

4. Analytics and performance

Nestwise may use analytics tools to understand general website activity, such as page views, device type, approximate region, traffic source, browser type, and interaction patterns. This information may be used to improve website performance and usability.

Analytics information is generally used in an aggregated or operational way and does not mean Nestwise performs wildlife services directly.

5. Advertising and referral measurement

Nestwise may use cookies, pixels, or similar tools to understand how visitors arrive at the website, measure advertising performance, and improve the matching request flow.

These technologies may be provided by third-party platforms or advertising partners. The use of such tools may be subject to those third parties’ own policies.

6. Third-party technologies

Some cookies or similar technologies may be set by third-party services used for analytics, security, lead processing, advertising measurement, form handling, hosting, or website functionality.

Nestwise does not control every third-party technology after a visitor interacts with an external provider, lead partner, advertising network, or third-party website.

7. Managing cookies

Most browsers allow visitors to block, delete, or manage cookies through browser settings. Browser controls may vary depending on the device, browser version, and operating system.

Blocking certain cookies may affect website functionality, including form behavior, preference storage, policy notice display, or request flow features.

8. Do Not Track signals

Some browsers may send “Do Not Track” signals. Because there is not a uniform technical standard for responding to these signals, Nestwise may not respond to them in a consistent manner.

9. Matching platform reminder

Cookies and tracking technologies support the website experience and matching request flow. They do not mean Nestwise performs wildlife removal, inspection, exclusion, repair, or cleanup services directly.

10. Changes to this Cookie Policy

Nestwise may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Updates may be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
