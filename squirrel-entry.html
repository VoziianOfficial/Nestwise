Request context

Squirrel entry concerns often begin with small gaps and quick sounds.

Providers may ask about fast movement in the attic, chewing sounds, roofline access, nearby trees, loose vents, soffit gaps, or small exterior openings that may need inspection.

Useful request details

Information that can help providers review squirrel entry concerns.

Sounds

Fast movement or chewing

Mention when sounds happen and whether they seem to come from the attic, roofline, wall, or soffit area.

Entry areas

Small visible gaps

Loose vents, soffit gaps, fascia openings, damaged screens, or roofline gaps may be helpful to describe.

Access

Trees and roof access

Nearby branches, roof overhangs, and exterior access routes may affect provider review and prevention options.

Photos

Safe image context

If safe, photos of exterior gaps or roofline areas can support a clearer provider conversation.
