Platform notice

Nestwise is a matching platform. It does not perform wildlife removal services directly.

1. Overview

Nestwise is an independent wildlife provider matching platform that helps homeowners request connections with independent local providers. This website is not a wildlife removal company, contractor, inspection company, repair company, or direct service provider.

By using this website, submitting a form, clicking a phone link, or otherwise interacting with Nestwise, you acknowledge that your information may be used to help process your matching request and communicate with you about provider options.

2. Information we may collect

When you submit a request, Nestwise may collect information such as your name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, selected wildlife concern category, and message details about the activity you are noticing.

The website may also collect basic technical information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, approximate location based on service area information, referral source, and interaction data used to improve website functionality.

3. How information may be used

Information submitted through this website may be used to process matching requests, communicate with homeowners, route requests to independent providers when available, improve website performance, maintain records, prevent misuse, and support legal or compliance obligations.

Provider availability may vary by location, scheduling capacity, request type, and other factors. Submitting information does not guarantee that a provider will be available or that any provider will accept the request.

4. Sharing with independent providers

Nestwise may share request information with independent local providers, lead partners, service networks, or other third parties involved in processing the matching request. These providers are independent businesses and are not employees, agents, or contractors of Nestwise.

Any service discussion, inspection, pricing, estimate, scheduling, warranty, or work agreement is handled directly between the homeowner and the provider.

5. Homeowner responsibility

Homeowners are responsible for reviewing and verifying any provider before hiring. This includes checking licenses, insurance, qualifications, written estimates, service scope, local wildlife requirements, and any other details relevant to the work being performed.

6. Cookies and tracking technologies

Nestwise may use cookies, pixels, analytics tools, or similar technologies to support website functionality, understand website performance, remember policy consent choices, and improve the user experience.

For more information, please review the Cookie Policy.

7. Data retention

Nestwise may retain submitted information for as long as reasonably necessary to process requests, maintain business records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, and improve website operations.

8. Security

Nestwise uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect information. However, no website, transmission method, or storage system can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

9. Third-party websites and providers

This website may contain links or connections to third-party websites, providers, lead partners, or service networks. Nestwise is not responsible for the privacy practices, policies, services, or actions of third parties.

10. Children’s privacy

This website is intended for use by adults seeking homeowner-related provider matching. Nestwise does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Nestwise may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates may be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
